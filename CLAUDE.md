## Programme Structure

### Split
6-day PPL, two full cycles per week. Day 7 is rest.

`Push → Pull → Legs → Push → Pull → Legs → Rest`

### Phase 1 — Weeks 1–3 (45 min)
Habit formation. Conservative load. Form priority. No overhead pressing. 4 exercises per session.

### Phase 2 — Week 4+ (45 min)
Volume increase. Sets increase from 3 to 4 on compounds. Flat press introduced (pain-gate on shoulder). Overhead tricep extension added cautiously.

## Warm-ups (per session type)

**PUSH**
- Arm circles — 30 sec each direction — Small to large. Loosens shoulder capsule. Non-negotiable.
- Rear delt pull (light dumbbell) — 2 × 15 reps — Activates rotator cuff before pressing.
- Incline press warm-up set — 1 × 15 reps — 50% of working weight. Pattern, not fatigue.

**PULL**
- Cat-cow / thoracic rotation — 60 sec — Mobilises spine before hinging. Reduces lower back strain.
- Hip hinge bodyweight — 2 × 10 reps — Hands down legs, feel the hamstring load.
- Bent-over row warm-up set — 1 × 15 reps — 50% of working weight. Prime the lats.

**LEGS**
- Bodyweight squat — 2 × 15 reps — Slow descent. Pause at bottom. Wake up glutes and quads.
- Hip circles — 30 sec each leg — Full range of motion. Loosens hip flexors.
- Goblet squat warm-up set — 1 × 15 reps — Light dumbbell. Pattern warm-up before working sets.

## Progressive Overload Rules

| Trigger | Action |
|---|---|
| Hit top of rep range on all sets | Increase weight by 2.5kg next session |
| Miss bottom of rep range on 2+ sets | Hold weight, focus on form |
| 3 weeks at same weight | Force increase by 1.25kg regardless |
| Right shoulder discomfort on press | Drop to incline only, reduce load 10% |

## Right Shoulder Protocol

- Incline only until week 4
- Rear delt fly on every pull day, non-negotiable
- Flat press introduced at week 4 only if pain-free
- Overhead movements cautious in Phase 2
- Drop load 10% and revert to incline-only at first sign of discomfort
- Rear delt and rotator cuff strength is the long-term fix alongside pec development

## Design Constraints

- Single `index.html` file, no build step, no dependencies except Google Fonts
- Mobile-first, portrait layout, large touch targets (min 44px)
- Dark theme: background `#0d0d0f`, text `#e8e6e0`
